2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create, verify, and operate your account and listings.
  • To display Listing Business data in anonymized form publicly, and full form to active paying members.
  • To process payments and issue tax invoices.
  • To authenticate logins via OTP.
  • To verify data accuracy and investigate reports of incorrect data.
  • To maintain security audit logs.
  • To communicate service-related notices (we do not send unsolicited marketing without consent).

3. Consent and Purpose Limitation

We collect and process your data only for the purposes stated above and only with your consent, which you provide when you register and submit information. We will not use your data for any new, incompatible purpose without seeking fresh consent. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Section 7), subject to legal retention requirements.

4. How We Share Data

We share your data only as follows:

  • With Investors: A Listing Business's identity, financials, and contact details are revealed only to Investors holding an active paid membership, as the core function of the Platform and with the Listing Business's explicit consent at listing.
  • With service providers: Payment gateway (Razorpay), SMS/OTP provider, and hosting provider — only as needed to operate the Platform, under confidentiality obligations.
  • As required by law: If compelled by a valid legal order from a court or government authority.

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers in India. We implement reasonable security safeguards including: HTTPS/SSL encryption, hashed storage of one-time passwords, document storage in non-public directories accessible only through authenticated requests, time-limited access links, rate limiting, and access audit logging. While we take these measures, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as required to provide the service. After account closure, we retain certain data only as long as necessary to comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

7. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act

As a Data Principal, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erase your data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent to processing.
  • Grievance redressal — to raise complaints with our Grievance Officer.
  • Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in case of death or incapacity.

To exercise any right, contact our Grievance Officer (Section 9). We will respond within the timeframes prescribed by law.

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies and local browser storage necessary to keep you logged in and operate the Platform. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies for behavioural profiling. If we deploy analytics in future, we will update this policy and seek consent where required.
