How to Raise a Grievance

  1. Email the Grievance Officer with your registered name, mobile number, and a clear description of your complaint.
  2. Include any relevant payment ID, listing code, or screenshots.
  3. We will acknowledge your complaint within 24 hours.
  4. We will investigate and resolve the matter within the timeframe prescribed under applicable law (and in any case as expeditiously as possible).
  5. You will receive a written resolution to your registered email.

Reporting Incorrect Listing Data

Investors can report a listing they believe contains incorrect or misleading data directly from the listing page using the "Report" feature, or by emailing the Grievance Officer. Verified incorrect listings are removed, the responsible account may be suspended, and affected download slots are restored automatically.
