How it works

Companies publish verified data. Research members access it on subscription.

🏢 For Companies

1. Register with company PAN + mobile OTP
2. Publish CA-certified financials & 36-month turnover
3. Add company profile, directors & contact details
4. Optionally add a corporate profile video
5. Sign the no-securities declaration
6. Go live after verification — for research visibility

🔎 For Research Members

1. Register with PAN + mobile OTP
2. Accept the research-use declaration
3. Subscribe for research access
4. Browse & filter the verified company database
5. View full financials, profile & contact details
6. Download up to 5 company research profiles
