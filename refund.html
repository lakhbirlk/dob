Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

This policy explains when and how refunds and cancellations apply to fees paid on dataofbusiness.in. It applies to both Business listing fees and Investor membership fees. All payments are processed through our payment gateway, Razorpay.

1. Nature of the Service

DataOfBusiness.in provides access to digital information services — anonymized and full business financial data, and listing visibility. Because access to digital content is granted immediately upon payment, refunds are limited as described below. Please review the service carefully before paying.

2. Investor Membership Refunds

  • 7-day cooling-off window: If you have not downloaded any business profile and have not revealed the identity of any listing, you may request a full refund within 7 days of payment.
  • After access is used: Once you have downloaded any profile, revealed any identity, or your membership has been active for more than 7 days, the membership fee is non-refundable, as the digital service has been substantially consumed.
  • Low-inventory protection: If, at the time of your payment, the Platform had fewer than [10] live listings in total, you may request a full refund within 7 days even if you have browsed (but not downloaded) listings.

3. Business Listing Fee Refunds

  • Before going live: If your listing is rejected during our verification review, the listing fee is fully refunded.
  • After going live: Once your listing is approved and published, the listing fee is non-refundable, as the service (visibility to Investors) has commenced.
  • Voluntary removal: You may remove your listing at any time, but no pro-rata refund is provided for the unused listing period.

4. No Refund for Transaction Outcomes

The Platform charges only for information access and listing visibility. We do not charge any success fee, commission, or transaction fee, and we are not a party to any investment or loan. No refund is available based on the outcome of any investment, loan, or negotiation you enter into with another user, as such transactions occur entirely outside the Platform and at your own risk.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email [support@dataofbusiness.in] from your registered email address, with your registered mobile number, payment ID, and reason. We will review eligibility against this policy and respond within 3 business days.

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method through Razorpay within 7–10 business days. GST already remitted on a refunded transaction will be adjusted as permitted by law. Any payment-gateway charges, where non-recoverable, may be deducted from the refund amount.

7. Cancellation of Auto-Renewal

Memberships do not auto-renew by default. If auto-renewal is offered and enabled in future, you may cancel it anytime from your account before the renewal date to avoid the next charge.
